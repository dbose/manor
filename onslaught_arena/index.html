Onslaught! is our first game, initially released on the Chrome Webstore and now also available for iPad and Android tablets. It's a fast-paced, medieval fantasy shoot 'em up, often compared to old classics like Smash TV or Gauntlet.

Please play the demo and let us know what you think!
