Onslaught! Arena is a fast-paced medieval fantasy shoot 'em up, often compared to classics like Smash TV or Gauntlet. It was our first game, initially released with the Chrome Webstore on December 7th, 2010, and is now also available in the Mac App Store.

Let's play!

Brief history

Onslaught! Arena was one of the first commercial HTML5 games on the market. It has been seen in Game Developer magazine, won an award at GDC and has been made free and open source.
