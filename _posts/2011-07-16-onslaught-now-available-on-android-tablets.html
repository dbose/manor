Hot on the heels of our announcement of Onslaught! for iPad, we're now equally as thrilled to announce Onslaught! for Android. This is again without any additional code on our part, thanks to Game Closure's awesome technology. Read all about it in our previous article, "The road to a production-quality HTML5 game on iOS".

Unfortunately it's currently only compatible with Android tablets, but we are working on a version that will work with Android phones too. Follow us on Twitter to know be notified as soon as it's available!
